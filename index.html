The 'White:' and 'Black:' containers here represent the current location of the pieces. 'Move List:' denotes the moves played from the current location of the pieces. The 'Move List:' initially contains the last played move by the opponent.

To play a move, click two squares on the chessboard. The first clicked square is the current location of the piece and the second clicked square is the location to which the user would like to move the piece.

The user may require to play multiple moves to complete a puzzle.

The user may click on this popup to close it.
